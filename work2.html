Start by creating a daily routine of two or three health promoting activities. On each day you successfully complete the activities award yourself a paper clip and attach it to your Chain of Paperclips.
The chain of paperclips will be a daily reminder that you are doing something
to improve your heath.
If you miss a day don't start over. Just keep building your chain of positive days. Select two or three You can starting activities
