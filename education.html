Lifestyle Diseases

Currently we are spending over 3 Trillion Dollars a year on treatment for all medical conditions. Over 80 percent of the money is for lifestyle diseases. Lifestyle diseases are preventable and in many cases reversable by changing one's daily habits.

Lifestyle Medicine

Incidences of Lifestyle diseases are increasing in number and the age of onset of these diseases is decreasing.

Adoption of the six pillars of lifestyle medicine can relieve the symptoms and in many cases reverse the progress of these diseases. It can also improve the quality of life for those suffering from non-lifestyle challenges such as trauma and disability.
This means you can adopt habits which improve your health and discard habits which harm your health. The six pillars provide a framework you can use to organize your activities.

Learn more about the six pillars of lifestyle medicine.

You can Start Now to improve your life.
Begin by creating a daily routine of two or three health promoting activities.
On each day you successfully complete the activities award yourself a paper clip and attach it to your Chain of Paperclips.
The chain of paperclips will be a daily reminder that you are doing something to improve your heath.
If you miss a day don't start over, just keep building your chain of positive days.
Select two or three starting activities from the pillars of lifestyle medicine you are confident you can complete.
